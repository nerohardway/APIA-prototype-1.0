Home & Content

Cover for loss or damage to your home and its contents against events such as fire, theft, storm, flood#, and vandalism

Plus a range of additional features and covers, including


Up to 52 weeks temporary accommodation if your home is unliveable after an insured event

Motor burnout cover (up to $2,000)

Loss of refrigerated food and medicines (up to $500)

Lock replacement if your house keys are stolen (up to $1,000)

Financial transaction card fraud if your card is stolen from your insured home (up to $1,500)

Visitor's contents cover (up to $2,000)

#Actions or movements of the sea and storm surge are not covered (unless the storm surge damage occurs at the same time as damage caused by storm) other exclusions apply.
